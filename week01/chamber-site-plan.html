Jason Rusk

WDD 230: Web Frontend Development I

Site Name

Elk Grove Chamber of Commerce

This name represents the city in which the chamber operates and promotes local businesses.

Site Purpose

  • To provide information about the services and resources offered by the Elk Grove Chamber of Commerce to support local businesses and promote economic growth in the community.
  • To serve as a platform for members to connect, network, and collaborate, fostering a strong and vibrant business community in Elk Grove.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming events is the chamber hosting to facilitate business-to-business networking and professional development?
  • Where can I find a directory of chamber members to explore potential partnerships and collaborations?
  • What resources and support does the chamber provide to help new businesses get established and thrive in Elk Grove?
  • How can I join the Elk Grove Chamber of Commerce, and what are the different membership levels and benefits, such as directory listings, publications, trainings, social media support, luncheons, sponsorships, and advertising?

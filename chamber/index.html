About Elk Grove Chamber of Commerce

The Chamber of Commerce is committed to being an active contributing member in our community. We are proud of the many businesses that support our service agencies, schools, youth athletic leagues, and non-profit organizations that make our wonderful community such a desirable place to live, work, and do business. As we look to the future, we want to continue to build on our successes and be a partner in the growth of our community.

We want to showcase Elk Grove in order to attract more businesses and jobs while enhancing our quality of life. The Chamber, as the voice of our businesses, works diligently to provide outstanding services for all of our members.
